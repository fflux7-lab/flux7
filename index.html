ABOUT FLUX7

NOTHING STAYS STILL.
NEITHER DO WE.

FLUX7 started in a two-person workshop with one rule: make clothes for people who are always mid-motion. No seasonal gimmicks, no filler pieces — just heavyweight fabrics, honest construction, and a tag that tells you the truth about what's in your hands.

Every FLX style is numbered, tracked, and made in small batches. When it's gone, the number retires.
